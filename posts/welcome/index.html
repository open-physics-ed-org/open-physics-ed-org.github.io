Welcome to Open Physics Education

January 2026 · 1 min · danny caballero

Welcome to our new site! We’re excited to share open physics education resources with you.

What We Do#

Open Physics Education is dedicated to creating and sharing high-quality physics resources that are:

  • Free and Open: All materials are freely available to use and modify
  • Accessible: Created with diverse learners in mind
  • Comprehensive: Covering fundamental physics through advanced topics
  • Community-Driven: Built by educators and physicists worldwide

Getting Started#

Explore our resources and join our community. Whether you’re a student learning physics for the first time or an educator looking for classroom materials, we have something for you.

Stay tuned for more updates and resources!
