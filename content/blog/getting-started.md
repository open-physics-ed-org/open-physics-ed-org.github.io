This post introduces our collection of physics education resources and how to get the most out of them.

## Organization

Our resources are organized by topic and difficulty level:

- **Mechanics**: Classical and modern mechanics
- **Thermodynamics**: Heat and energy
- **Waves & Optics**: Wave phenomena and light
- **Electromagnetism**: Electric and magnetic fields
- **Modern Physics**: Quantum mechanics and relativity

## Using These Resources

All materials are available in multiple formats:

- **Web Format**: View directly in your browser
- **PDF**: Download for offline use
- **Source Files**: Modify and adapt for your needs

## Contributing

Found an error? Want to improve a resource? All our materials are open source. Visit our GitHub repository to contribute.

Happy learning!
